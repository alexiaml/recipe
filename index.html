Raised Waffles

From The Boston Cooking-School Cook Book

By Fannie Merritt Farmer



These Raised Waffles are different from ordinary waffles. They require yeast cake (or fresh yeast) which allows waffle batter to double in volume and it creates a lighter yet still flavorful finished product.



Ingredients:

  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ yeast cake (fresh yeast)
  • ¼ cup lukewarm water
  • 2 cups flour
  • Yolks 2 eggs
  • Whites 2 eggs

Directions:

  1. Scald milk; add salt and butter, and when lukewarm, add yeast cake dissolved in water, and flour.
  2. Beat well; let rise over night; add yolks of eggs well beaten, and whites of eggs beaten stiff.
  3. Cook as Waffles.
  4. By using a whole yeast cake, the mixture will rise in one and one-half hours.
